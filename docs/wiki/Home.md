## What Zeref OS does in one paragraph

Per-project flat `memory/` wiki in plain markdown, append-only `PATTERNS.jsonl` event log, point-in-time snapshots, contradiction safety with human arbitration, three privacy modes (`exact` / `abstract` / `local-only`), six on-demand team packs, and a cross-harness handoff format. AI sessions become cumulative across every harness you use.
